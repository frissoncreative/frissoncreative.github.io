Clients and Projects

Social Media and Community Management

Our team of highly skilled social media experts enable us to offer a range of community management services, extending from one-off event support through to 24/7 account takeovers and long term campaigns.

Whether our clients are looking to grow their followers, increase their engagement or raise brand awareness, we are ideally positioned to support and manage this process in the most effective way.

Influencer Marketing

We connect brands with their target audiences through the most relevant and effective social influencers.

Our extensive network consists of the leading sports and football accounts on Twitter, with highly engaged audiences.

We manage the entire process in-house, from ideation and talent management, through content production and activation, right up to final reporting and analysis.

we deliver campaigns tailored to achieve our client’s desired outcomes.

Online and traditional PR

We go beyond social channels to distribute our client’s key brand messages and campaigns through both traditional offline media and digital platforms.

We utilise our established journalist relationships to secure coverage, links and tagline in prominent media areas.

Our services also cover blogger outreach and the creation of engaging and shareable content assets which we use to reach out to a comprehensive network of highly respected bloggers.

Content creation

Our in-house design team produce high quality, tailored graphics, videos and downloadable assets that showcase our client's ranges in both a unique and relevant way.

We work closely with our clients to create custom, branded materials designed to be shared and to take reach beyond a core audience, to attract and engage new demographics.

Video production and live streaming

Our suite of social video production and publishing tools allows our brands to activate global audiences and deliver a truly immersive and engaging viewer experience.

We provide live streaming to all major social networks by utilising HD broadcast standard cameras and industry standard encoders and our offerings range from single cam productions, all the way up to multi-camera setups with graphical overlays.
